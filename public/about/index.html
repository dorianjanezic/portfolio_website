about projects

Dorian Janežič is a creative technologist and a music producer focusing on interactive experiences for artistic, cultural and educative applications. Originally trained as a digital communication specialist he has worked in maritime industry to develop functional digital products.

He attended NYU Tisch School of the Arts IMA Low Res, a graduate program focusing on experimental and artistic uses of technology. Throughout the program he developed his own artistic voice and through interactive projects he asks questions of contemporary problems on environmental and societal level.

His current artistic research explores how can sound help us understand and identify the healthiness of the surrounding environments and ecosystems.
